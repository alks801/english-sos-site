Privacy Policy

Application: English SOS / “Phrasebook”
Last updated: 2026-01-17

1. Data Collection

The “Phrasebook” application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. All data (such as favorite phrases and settings) is stored only on your device.

2. Local Storage

The application may store the following data on your device:

  • List of favorite phrases
  • Onboarding completion status
  • Pro version purchase status

This data is not transmitted to any external servers and is used only within the application.

3. In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed through the App Store (Apple). We do not have access to your payment details. To verify purchase status and restore purchases, the RevenueCat service may be used. RevenueCat does not share your payment information with us and is used only to confirm your purchase status.

4. Analytics

The application does not use any third-party analytics services and does not track user activity.

5. Text-to-Speech

The text-to-speech feature uses the speech synthesizer built into your device. Text is not sent to any external servers.

6. Permissions

The application does not require special permissions and does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this address.

For privacy-related questions, please visit the support page.
