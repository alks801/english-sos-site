Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the English SOS application (“the App”) handles user data. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data of users. All information such as saved phrases, onboarding status, and app settings is stored locally on your device only.

2. Local Storage

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • List of favorite phrases
  • Onboarding completion status
  • Pro version purchase status

This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any external servers.

3. In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed through the App Store (Apple). We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase status is managed via RevenueCat only for entitlement validation.

4. Analytics

The App does not use any third-party analytics services and does not track user behavior.

5. Text-to-Speech

The text-to-speech feature uses the built-in speech synthesizer of your device. No text data is sent to external servers.

6. Permissions

The App does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the App.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support page.
